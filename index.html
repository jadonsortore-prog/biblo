Our Story

Serving Dallas with Excellence Since 2009

Lone Star Plumbing was founded by Master Plumber James Mitchell with a simple mission: deliver honest, high-quality plumbing services that Dallas families can trust. What started as a one-man operation has grown into a team of over 50 dedicated professionals.

As a family-owned business, we understand the importance of treating every customer's home like our own. We've built our reputation one job at a time — through transparent pricing, exceptional craftsmanship, and a genuine commitment to our community.

Today, we're proud to be one of Dallas's highest-rated plumbing companies, with thousands of five-star reviews from homeowners across the metroplex.

Drain Cleaning & Unclogging

Our drain cleaning services restore flow quickly and effectively using industry-leading techniques:

  • High-pressure hydro jetting to blast away grease, scale, and debris
  • Motorized drain snaking for stubborn clogs
  • Video camera inspection to pinpoint the exact location and cause
  • Root intrusion removal and prevention
  • Kitchen, bathroom, floor, and main line drain cleaning
  • Preventive maintenance plans to avoid future blockages

Water Heater Services

Complete water heater solutions for consistent hot water and energy savings:

  • Traditional tank water heater installation (40, 50, 75 gallon)
  • Tankless water heater installation and conversion
  • Thermostat and element replacement
  • Anode rod replacement to extend tank life
  • Expansion tank installation
  • Energy-efficient upgrade consultations
  • Annual maintenance and flushing services

Pipe Repair & Replacement

Expert pipe repair and full repiping services for your home:

  • Electronic and acoustic leak detection
  • Copper, PEX, and CPVC pipe installation
  • Whole-house repiping for older galvanized systems
  • Slab leak repair and rerouting
  • Burst pipe emergency repair
  • Frozen pipe thawing and insulation
  • Gas line repair and installation

Toilet & Fixture Repair

Professional fixture services for every room in your home:

  • Toilet installation, repair, and replacement
  • Faucet installation and repair (kitchen & bath)
  • Sink installation and drain repair
  • Showerhead and bathtub fixture upgrades
  • Garbage disposal installation and repair
  • Water-saving fixture upgrades
  • Outdoor hose bib and spigot repair

Bathroom & Kitchen Remodeling

Full plumbing services for your remodeling project from planning to completion:

  • Pre-construction plumbing design and consultation
  • Rough-in plumbing for new layouts
  • Fixture selection guidance and installation
  • Shower and tub installation including tile-ready pans
  • Kitchen sink and dishwasher plumbing
  • Water line relocation and extension
  • Coordination with general contractors and inspectors

Sewer Line Services

Comprehensive sewer line solutions using the latest technology:

  • Video camera sewer line inspection
  • Trenchless sewer repair (pipe bursting & pipe lining)
  • Traditional sewer line replacement
  • Sewer line cleaning and root removal
  • Sewer line locate services
  • Backflow prevention device installation
  • Septic system connections

Leak Detection & Repair

Pinpoint and fix leaks before they cause costly damage:

  • Electronic leak detection equipment
  • Acoustic listening devices for hidden leaks
  • Thermal imaging for behind-wall leaks
  • Slab leak detection and repair
  • Water meter leak testing
  • Pool and spa leak detection
  • Water damage mitigation guidance

Emergency Plumbing — 24/7

When disaster strikes, our emergency team is ready 24 hours a day, 7 days a week:

  • Burst and broken pipe repair
  • Sewage backup cleanup and repair
  • Flooding and water extraction support
  • Gas leak detection and repair
  • Overflowing toilets and fixtures
  • No-hot-water emergencies
  • Typical response time: under 60 minutes
